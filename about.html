Years of experience

Sophie Clasen has spent many years working in stables, producing young horses for jumping and dressage. Working in the equestrian business in Holland, Belgium, Italy and Germany she was trained by well known riders and their system.
Our system

Having worked at many different stables she learned to develop horses in many different systems. By taking a little bit from all the systems, she has created her own training system that gives proven results.

Groundwork

Our horses are trained a few times a week in the longe or by hand with a cavasson. This is a way of training that softens the muscles and strengthens the horses` top line. We find this very favourable for jumping and dressage horses as well as young horses that need to find their balance and build a top line.
