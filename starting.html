Starting young horses

We offer breaking and starting of young stallions, mares and geldings.

Building blocks

The training of each horse starts with gaining trust. We believe that trust and respect is the foundation for everything the horse will be asked in his later life.


Gaining focus

After gaining their trust and respect, the training continues whilst keeping their attention. Once they have our attention and trust, we introduce everything they are faced with on competitions in the beginning to make the horses relaxed and focused on the trainer.

Mission
When the horses trust and respect their leader, focus on their job and understand what they are asked, they will work their hearts out unconditionally. Our mission is to produce confident and positive horses that are enjoying their job and mission in life.


Collaboration

When training a horse for an owner, we believe in a system that is favorable for all three parts involved. The horse should enjoy his new job and future ad the owner should have a favorable outcome. When the horse enjoys learning and the owner is happy with the progress, we as the third part will be satisfied.
