Selecting and producing

SChorses is an equestrian company focusing on selecting and producing young and promising horses.

Long-time collaboration

We believe in quality partnerships and long-time collaborations that are favorable for horse, owner, trainer and investor.
Quality production

We train each horse individually and make sure every horse get the best care that they deserve.

Principles:

Based on the German training scale.

Develop positive horses willing to learn.

Keep the joy of training for the horse.

Make the horses curios for further learning.

Develop soft and supple muscles that protects the horses` joints and tendons.


Presenting horses

We make sure that the horses we have for sale look presentable at all times, in the stable as well as on pictures.

Harmonius training

All our horses are making progress with reliable results. They are trained in a system based on clear and consequent signals, in a stress-free environment. The horses in our training system are free of force and therefore they keep their natural curiosity and willingness to learn. Stress limits the brains capacity to take in new knowledge. Both humans and horses. We therefore make sure that the training of our horses is always in a calm and harmonious atmosphere.

Do not hesitate to contact us for a talk about your horse, and what we can offer.
